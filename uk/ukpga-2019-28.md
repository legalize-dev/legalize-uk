# Census (Return Particulars and Removal of Penalties) Act 2019

#### Amendment of the Census Act 1920

##### 1

- (1) The Census Act 1920 is amended as follows.

- (2) In the Schedule (matters in respect of which particulars may be required), after paragraph 5A insert—

> (5B) Sexual orientation.
> (5C) Gender identity.

- (3) In section 8 (penalties), in subsection (1A), at the end insert “, sexual orientation or gender identity.”

#### Amendment of the Census Act (Northern Ireland) 1969

##### 2

- (1) The Census Act (Northern Ireland) 1969 is amended as follows.

- (2) In the Schedule (matters in respect of which particulars may be required), after paragraph 7 insert—

> (7A) Sexual orientation.
> (7B) Gender identity.

- (3) In section 7 (penalties for offences), in subsection (3), after “profession” insert “or any particulars with respect to sexual orientation or gender identity”.

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 3

- (1) Section 1 extends to England and Wales.

- (2) Section 2 extends to Northern Ireland.

- (3) This section extends to England and Wales and to Northern Ireland.

- (4) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (5) This Act may be cited as the Census (Return Particulars and Removal of Penalties) Act 2019.
