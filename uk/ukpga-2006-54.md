# Consolidated Fund Act 2006 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2007

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2007 is authorised to the amount of £6,284,598,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2007

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2007 the sum of £7,831,205,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2008

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2008 is authorised to the amount of £181,206,923,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2008

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2008 the sum of £171,368,021,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2006.
