# Appropriation (No. 2) Act 2002 (repealed)

#### Non-operating appropriations in aid for 2001-02

##### 1

- (1) The Schedule to this Act sets out modifications of the limits set for the purposes of section 2 of the Government Resources and Accounts Act [2000 (c. 20)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2000/20) on the resources applicable, in accordance with that Act, as non-operating appropriations in aid for the year 2001-02.

- (2) The limits, as modified by the Schedule to this Act, shall be deemed to have been in force throughout the year 2001-02.

- (3) Subsection (2), so far as it relates to the reduction of a limit, does not affect the validity of any direction given by the Treasury under section 2 of the Government Resources and Accounts Act 2000 during the year 2001-02.

- (4) In this section “the year 2001-02” means the year that ended with 31st March 2002.

#### Short title

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the Appropriation (No. 2) Act 2002.
