# Local Government Act 2010

#### Prevention of implementation of certain proposals for single tier of local government

##### 1

- (1) After the commencement of this Act, no further relevant order may be made under section 7 of the 2007 Act (power to implement proposals for a single tier of local government for an area).

- (2) In this section—

- a “relevant order” means an order implementing (with or without modifications) a proposal received by the Secretary of State before the commencement of this Act;

- “the 2007 Act” means the Local Government and Public Involvement in Health Act 2007.

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act extends to England and Wales only.

- (2) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act may be cited as the Local Government Act 2010.
