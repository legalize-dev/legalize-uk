# Theatres Trust (Scotland) Act 1978

#### Extension of Theatres Trust Act 1976 to Scotland.

##### 1

- (1) The Theatres Trust Act 1976 shall be amended as follows, and as so amended shall extend to Scotland and come into force there on the passing of this Act.

- (2) In section 2 (objects of Trust and powers of trustees), after subsection (2) there shall be added—

> (3) Subsection (2) above shall have effect in relation to Scotland with the substitution for paragraph (d) of the following paragraph—
>   (d) from time to time to sell, let, surrender or otherwise dispose of, or apply as security for borrowing, any heritable or moveable property vested in or acquired by the trustees;

.

- (3) In section 6(4) (extent), the words " Scotland or " are hereby repealed.

#### Short title and extent.

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Theatres Trust (Scotland) Act 1978.

- (2) This Act does not extend to Northern Ireland.
