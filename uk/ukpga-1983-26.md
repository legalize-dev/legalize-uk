# Pet Animals Act 1951 (Amendment) Act 1983

#### Amendment of 1951 Act.

##### 1

The Pet Animals Act 1951 shall be amended as follows:—

- (a) in section 2 (Pets not to be sold in streets, etc.), there shall be substituted for the word “except” the word “or”;

- (b) in subsection (3) of section 7 (Interpretation) there shall be omitted the words

> “*premises*” includes any stall or barrow in a market, but save as aforesaid does not include any stall or barrow or any part of a street or public place

.

#### Short title, commencement and extent.

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Pet Animals Act 1951 (Amendment) Act 1982.

- (2) This Act shall come into operation at the end of a period of six months beginning with the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act does not apply to Northern Ireland.
