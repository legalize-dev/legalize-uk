# European Union (Approvals) Act 2014 (repealed)

#### Approval of draft decisions under Article 352 of TFEU

##### 1

- (1) This section has effect for the purposes of section 8 of the European Union Act 2011 (decisions under Article 352 of TFEU).

- (2) The following draft decisions of the Council of the European Union under Article 352 of TFEU are approved—

- (a) the draft decision to adopt the Council Regulation on the deposit of the historical archives of the institutions at the European University Institute in Florence (document number 6867/13), and

- (b) the draft decision to adopt the Council Regulation establishing for the period 2014-2020 the programme “Europe for Citizens” (document number 12557/13).

- (3) In this section “TFEU” means the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union.

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act extends to the whole of the United Kingdom.

- (2) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act may be cited as the European Union (Approvals) Act 2014.
