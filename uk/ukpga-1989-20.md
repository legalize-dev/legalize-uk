# Licensing (Amendment) Act 1989

#### Modification of permitted hours

##### 1

In section 62(1)(b)(i) of the Licensing Act 1964 (permitted hours in clubs) for the word “five” there shall be substitued the word “six”.

#### Short title, commencement and extent

##### 2

—

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Licensing (Amendment) Act 1989.

- (2) This Act shall come into force at the end of the period of two months beginning with the date on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act extends to England and Wales only.
