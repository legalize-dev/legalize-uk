# Consolidated Fund Act 2008 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31 March 2009

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31 March 2009 is authorised to the amount of £7,425,726,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31 March 2009

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31 March 2009 the sum of £32,112,484,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31 March 2010

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31 March 2010 is authorised to the amount of £202,426,196,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31 March 2010

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31 March 2010 the sum of £194,439,006,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2008.
