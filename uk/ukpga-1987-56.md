# Scottish Development Agency Act 1987 (repealed 1. 4. 1991)

#### Increase of financial limit.

##### 1

In section 13(3) of the Scottish Development Agency Act 1975 (which sets out the limit on the aggregate of certain amounts outstanding as regards the Scottish Development Agency), for the words “£700 million” (which were substituted by section 2(1) of the Industry Act 1981) there shall be substituted the words “ £1,200 million ”.

#### Short title and extent.

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Scottish Development Agency Act 1987.

- (2) This Act extends to Scotland only.
