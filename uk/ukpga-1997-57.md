# Appropriation (No. 2) Act 1997

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1998

##### 1

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1998 the sum of £66,285,000. Appropriation of Grants

#### Appropriation of sums voted for supply services

##### 2

The sum granted by this Act out of the said Consolidated Fund towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty amounting, as appears by Schedule (A) annexed to this Act, to the sum of £66,285,000 is appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in Schedule (B) annexed hereto.

#### Short title

##### 3

This Act may be cited as the Appropriation (No. 2) Act 1997.
