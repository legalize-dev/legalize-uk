# Gaming (Amendment) Act 1987 (repealed)

#### Amendment of section 18 of the Gaming Act 1968.

##### 1

- (1) Section 18 of the Gaming Act 1968 (gaming on Sundays) shall be amended as follows.

- (2) For subsection (1) there shall be substituted the following—

> (1) In England and Wales no gaming shall take place on any Sunday between the hours of four in the morning and two in the afternoon on any premises in respect of which a licence under this Act is for the time being in force
