# Consolidated Fund Act 2004 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year that ended with 31st March 2003

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year that ended with 31st March 2003 is authorised to the amount of £15,975,062.02.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year that ended with 31st March 2003

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year that ended with 31st March 2003 the sum of £1,188.38.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2004

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2004 is authorised to the amount of £27,078,400,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2004

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2004 the sum of £10,668,823,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2004.
