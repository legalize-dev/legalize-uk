# Leasehold Reform (Amendment) Act 2014

#### Amendment of the Leasehold Reform, Housing and Urban Development Act 1993

##### 1

In section 99(5)(a) of the Leasehold Reform, Housing and Urban Development Act 1993 (requirement for notice under section 13 or 42 of that Act to be signed by tenants or tenant personally) after “42” insert “in respect of premises in Wales”.

#### Short title, commencement and extent

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Leasehold Reform (Amendment) Act 2014.

- (2) This Act comes into force at the end of the period of two months beginning with the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act extends to England and Wales only.
