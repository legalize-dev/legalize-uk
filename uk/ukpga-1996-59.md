# Public Order (Amendment) Act 1996 (repealed)

#### Power of arrest

##### 1

Section 5(4)(a) of the Public Order Act 1986 shall be amended by leaving out the word “the” and inserting the word “a”.

#### Citation

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the Public Order (Amendment) Act 1996.
