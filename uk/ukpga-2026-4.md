# Licensing Hours Extensions Act 2026

#### Procedure for making licensing hours orders

##### 1

In section 197 of the Licensing Act 2003 (regulations and orders)—

- (a) in subsection (3), omit paragraph (d);

- (b) in subsection (4), omit “(d),”;

- (c) omit subsection (5).

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act extends to England and Wales only.

- (2) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act may be cited as the Licensing Hours Extensions Act 2026.
