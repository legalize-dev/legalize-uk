# Local Government Act 1978

#### Power for authorities to incur expenditure to remedy injustices caused by maladministration.

##### 1

At the end of section 31 of the Local Government Act 1974 and section 29 of the Local Government (Scotland) Act 1975 there shall be added the following subsection:—

> (3) In any case where—
>   (a) a report is laid before an authority under subsection (1) above, and
>   (b) on consideration of the report, it appears to the authority that a payment should be made to, or some other benefit should be provided for, a person who has suffered injustice in consequence of maladministration referred to in the report,
> the authority may incur such expenditure as appears to them to be appropriate in making such a payment or providing such a benefit.

#### Short title and extent.

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Local Government Act 1978.

- (2) This Act does not extend to Northern Ireland.
