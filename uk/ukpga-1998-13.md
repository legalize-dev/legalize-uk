# Animal Health (Amendment) Act 1998

#### Welfare of animals kept in quarantine

##### 1

In Schedule 2 to the Animal Health Act 1981 (matters in respect of which importation control orders may be made), after paragraph 4 there shall be inserted the following paragraph—

> (4A) The premises at which animals and other things are to be kept in quarantine in pursuance of the order and the welfare of the animals kept in such premises.

.

#### Short title, commencement and extent

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Animal Health (Amendment) Act 1998.

- (2) This Act shall come into force at the end of the period of two months beginning with the date on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act does not extend to Northern Ireland.
