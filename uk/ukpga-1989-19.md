# International Parliamentary Organisations (Registration) Act 1989

#### Register

##### 1

- (1) The Secretary of State shall lay before Parliament each financial year a register of organisations listed in the Schedule to this Act, these being publicly-financed international parliamentary organisations which are in receipt of an annual grant-in-aid to fund both British and international secretariats and which draw their membership from both Houses of Parliament.

- (2) The register shall include the following—

- (a) the name of the organisation; and

- (b) the amount of money allocated to that organisation in that financial year.

#### Short title

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the International Parliamentary Organisations (Registration) Act 1989.
