# Consolidated Fund Act 1995 (repealed 21.3.1997)

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ended 31st March 1994

##### 1

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ended on 31st March 1994 the sum of £167,222,897.35.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1995

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1995 the sum of £1,171,475,000.

#### Short title

##### 3

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 1995.
