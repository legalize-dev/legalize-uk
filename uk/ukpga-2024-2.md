# Northern Ireland (Executive Formation) Act 2024

#### Extension of period for making Ministerial appointments

##### 1

In section 1(1) of the Northern Ireland (Executive Formation etc) Act 2022, for “18 January 2024” substitute “8 February 2024”.

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

- (2) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act may be cited as the Northern Ireland (Executive Formation) Act 2024.
