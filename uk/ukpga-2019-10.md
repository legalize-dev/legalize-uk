# Children Act 1989 (Amendment) (Female Genital Mutilation) Act 2019

#### Amendment to the Children Act 1989

##### 1

- (1) The Children Act 1989 is amended as follows.

- (2) In section 8 (child arrangements orders and other orders with respect to children), after subsection (4)(i) insert—

> (j) Part 1 of Schedule 2 to the Female Genital Mutilation Act 2003 (other than paragraph 3 of that Schedule).

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act extends to England and Wales only.

- (2) This Act comes into force on the day on which it is passed.

- (3) This Act may be cited as the Children Act 1989 (Amendment) (Female Genital Mutilation) Act 2019.
