# Pensions Dashboards (Prohibition of Indemnification) Act 2023

#### No indemnification for penalties under pensions dashboards regulations

##### 1

- (1) In section 256 of the Pensions Act 2004 (no indemnification for fines or civil penalties), in subsection (1)(b), for “or 88A” substitute “, 88A or 238G”.

- (2) In Article 233 of the Pensions (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 ([S.I. 2005/255 (N.I. 1)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisi/2005/255)) (no indemnification for fines or civil penalties), in paragraph (1)(b), for “or 83A” substitute “, 83A or 215G”.

#### Extent, commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) Section 1[(1)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/1/1/enacted) extends to England and Wales and Scotland.

- (2) Section 1[(2)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/1/2/enacted) extends to Northern Ireland.

- (3) This section extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

- (4) Section 1[(1)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/1/1/enacted) comes into force on such day as the Secretary of State may by regulations appoint.

- (5) Section 1[(2)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/1/2/enacted) comes into force on such day as the Department for Communities in Northern Ireland may by order appoint.

- (6) This section comes into force on the day on which this Act is passed.

- (7) Regulations made by the Secretary of State under subsection [(4)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/2/4/enacted) are to be made by statutory instrument.

- (8) The power of the Department for Communities in Northern Ireland to make an order under subsection [(5)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/14/section/2/5/enacted) is exercisable by statutory rule for the purposes of the Statutory Rules (Northern Ireland) Order 1979 ([S.I. 1979/1573 (N.I. 12)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisi/1979/1573)).

- (9) This Act may be cited as the Pensions Dashboards (Prohibition of Indemnification) Act 2023.
