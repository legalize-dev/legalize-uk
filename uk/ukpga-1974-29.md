# Parks Regulation (Amendment) Act 1974

##### 1

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Parks Regulation (Amendment) Act 1974.

- (2) The Parks Regulation Act 1872 and the Parks Regulation (Amendment) Act 1926 and this Act may be cited together as the Parks Regulation Acts 1872 to 1974.

##### 2

The Parks Regulation Act 1872 shall have effect subject to the amendments set out in the Schedule to this Act.

## SCHEDULE

##### 1

In sections 3, 5, 6, 7 and 8 for the words "park-keeper" or " keeper ", there shall be substituted the words " park constable " .

##### 2

After section 3 there shall be inserted the following section:—

> (3A) Every park constable shall on appointment be attested as a constable by making a declaration before a justice of the peace that he will duly execute the office of constable.
