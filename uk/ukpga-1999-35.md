# Consolidated Fund (No.2) Act 1999 (repealed)

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 2000

##### 1

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 2000 the sum of £3,773,575,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 2001

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 2001 the sum of £107,974,477,000.

#### Short title

##### 3

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund (No. 2) Act 1999.
