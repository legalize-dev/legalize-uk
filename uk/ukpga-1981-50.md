# Friendly Societies Act 1981 (repealed)

#### Repeal of 1974 c.46 s. 2(1).

##### 1

Section 2(1) of the Friendly Societies Act 1974 (qualification of Chief Registrar) is hereby repealed.

#### Short title and extent.

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Friendly Societies Act 1981.

- (2) This Act extends to the Isle of Man and the Channel Islands but does not extend to Northern Ireland.
