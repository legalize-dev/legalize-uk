# International Sugar Organisation Act 1973

#### Application of s. 1 of the International Organisations Act 1968.

##### 1

The termination of the membership of Her Majesty’s Government in the United Kingdom of the International Sugar Organisation shall not affect the application to that Organisation of section 1 of the International Organisations Act 1968.

#### Short title.

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the International Sugar Organisation Act 1973.
