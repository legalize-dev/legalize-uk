# Consolidated Fund Act 2005 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2006

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2006 is authorised to the amount of £3,183,848,000.

#### Issue of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2006

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2006 the sum of £4,677,710,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2007

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2007 is authorised to the amount of £171,791,003,000.

#### Issue of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2007

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2007 the sum of £161,256,893,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2005.
