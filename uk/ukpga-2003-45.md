# Consolidated Fund (No. 2) Act 2003 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2004

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2004 is authorised to the amount of £6,616,861,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2004

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2004 the sum of £7,324,511,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2005

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2005 is authorised to the amount of £147,233,877,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2005

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2005 the sum of £143,612,811,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund (No. 2) Act 2003.
