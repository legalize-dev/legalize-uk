# Consolidated Fund Act 2002 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending on 31st March 2002

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending on 31st March 2002 is authorised to the amount of £6,592,542,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending on 31st March 2001

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending on 31st March 2001 the sum of £3,097,059.33.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending on 31st March 2002

##### 3

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending on 31st March 2002 the sum of £5,367,457,000.

#### Short title

##### 4

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2002.
