# Welsh Development Agency Act 1997 (repealed)

#### Financial limit of Welsh Development Agency

##### 1

- (1) Section 18 of the Welsh Development Agency Act 1975 (finances of the Agency) is amended as follows.

- (2) In subsection (2) for “specified in” there is substituted “applicable under”.

- (3) In subsection (3) for “£950 million” there is substituted “£1,350 million or such greater sum as the Secretary of State may from time to time by order specify”.

- (4) After subsection (3) there is inserted—

> (3A) No order under subsection (3) above shall be made unless a draft of the order has been laid before, and approved by a resolution of, the House of Commons.

#### Short title, extent, commencement and repeal

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Welsh Development Agency Act 1997.

- (2) This Act does not extend to Northern Ireland.

- (3) This Act shall come into force at the end of the period of two months beginning with the day on which it is passed.

- (4) The Welsh Development Agency Act 1991 (which relates to the financial limit of the Agency and is superseded by section 1) is hereby repealed.
