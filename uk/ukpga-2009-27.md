# Consolidated Fund Act 2009

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31 March 2010

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31 March 2010 is authorised to the amount of £6,617,232,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31 March 2010

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31 March 2010 the sum of £23,998,577,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31 March 2011

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31 March 2011 is authorised to the amount of £218,175,405,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31 March 2011

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31 March 2011 the sum of £219,011,739,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2009.
