# Consolidated Fund Act 2003 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending on 31st March 2002

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending on 31st March 2002 is authorised to the amount of £1,504,912,617.37.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending on 31st March 2002

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending on 31st March 2002 the sum of £199,955,750.09.

#### Use of resources for the year ending on 31st March 2003

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending on 31st March 2003 is authorised to the amount of £21,387,488,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending on 31st March 2003

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending on 31st March 2003 the sum of £7,497,514,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2003.
