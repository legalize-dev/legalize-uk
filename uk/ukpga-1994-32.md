# Sale of Goods (Amendment) Act 1994

#### Repeal of s. 22(1) of the Sale of Goods Act 1979

##### 1

Section 22 (1) (relating to the sale of goods in market overt) of the Sale of Goods Act 1979 is hereby repealed.

#### Consequential Repeals

##### 2

- (1) Section 47 of the Laws in Wales Act 1542 (sale of stolen goods in a fair or market in Wales) is hereby repealed.

- (2) In section 7(3) of the Sea Fisheries (Shellfish) Act 1967 (protection of fisheries for shellfish), the words “sold in market overt or” shall be omitted.

#### Short title, commencement and extent

##### 3

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Sale of Goods (Amendment) Act 1994.

- (2) This Act shall apply to any contract for sale of goods which is made after this Act comes into force.

- (3) This Act shall come into force at the end of the period of two months beginning with the day on which it is passed.

- (4) Section 1 and this section extend to Northern Ireland.
