# Development Board for Rural Wales Act (Repealed 1.10.1998)

#### Increase of financial limit

##### 1

In section 12 of the Development of Rural Wales Act 1976 (financial limit for the Development Board for Rural Wales), for subsection (2) there shall be substituted—

> (2) The said limit is £175 million.

;

#### Short title

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the Development Board for Rural Wales Act 1991.
