# Consolidated Fund Act 2007 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2008

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2008 is authorised to the amount of £6,145,881,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2008

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2008 the sum of £7,535,362,000.

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2009

##### 3

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2009 is authorised to the amount of £194,866,358,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2009

##### 4

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2009 the sum of £179,676,962,000.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 2007.
