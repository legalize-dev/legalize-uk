# Fireworks Act 1964 (repealed)

#### Exemption of fireworks for export from requirements as to marking.

##### 1

Nothing in section 5(1) or (2) of the Fireworks Act 1951 (which impose requirements as to the marking of fireworks and their containers) shall apply to fireworks consigned from a factory for transmission to a place outside the United Kingdom, the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man.

#### Short title.

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the Fireworks Act 1964.
