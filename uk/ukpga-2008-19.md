# Appropriation (No. 3) Act 2008 (repealed)

#### Use of resources for the year ending with 31st March 2009

##### 1

The use of resources for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2009 is authorised to the amount of £1,000.

#### Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending with 31st March 2009

##### 2

The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply to the service of the year ending with 31st March 2009 the sum of £42,200,000,000.

#### Appropriation of amount and sum voted for a supply service

##### 3

The amount and sum authorised by this Act for the service of the year ending with 31st March 2009 is appropriated, and shall be deemed to have been appropriated as from 1st April 2008, for the service and purposes specified in the Schedule.

#### Short title

##### 4

This Act may be cited as the Appropriation (No. 3) Act 2008.
