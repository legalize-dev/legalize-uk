# Sea Fisheries (Shellfish) (Amendment) Act 1997

#### Power to make orders as to fisheries for lobsters and other crustaceans

##### 1

- (1) Section 1(1) of the Sea Fisheries (Shellfish) Act 1967 (which enables the Secretary of State to make orders as to fisheries for shellfish) is amended as follows.

- (2) After “clams” there is inserted “, lobsters”.

- (3) After “molluscs” there is inserted “or crustaceans”.

#### Short title and extent

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Sea Fisheries (Shellfish) (Amendment) Act 1997.

- (2) This Act does not extend to Northern Ireland.
