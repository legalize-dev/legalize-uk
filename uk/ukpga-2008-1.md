# European Communities (Finance) Act 2008 (repealed)

#### Extended meaning of “the Treaties” and “the Community Treaties”

##### 1

In section 1(2) of the European Communities Act [1972 (c. 68)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1972/68), in the definition of “the Treaties” and “the Community Treaties”, for paragraph (e) (Council decisions on Communities' system of own resources) substitute—

> (e) the decisions of the Council of 7th May 1985, 24th June 1988, 31st October 1994, 29th September 2000 and 7th June 2007 on the Communities' system of own resources;

.

#### Short title and repeal

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the European Communities (Finance) Act 2008.

- (2) The European Communities (Finance) Act [2001 (c. 22)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2001/22) (which is superseded by this Act) is repealed.
