# Assembly Members (Reduction of Numbers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2016

#### Reduction of number of members returned for each constituency

##### 1

- (1) In section 33(2) of the Northern Ireland Act 1998 (numbers of members returned for each constituency), for “six” substitute “five”.

- (2) The amendment made by subsection (1) does not have effect until the first Assembly election after the 2016 election.

- (3) In subsection (2)—

- “Assembly election” means an election under section 31 or 32 of the Northern Ireland Act 1998 (whichever occurs first), and

- “2016 election” means the election held in 2016 in accordance with section 31(1) of that Act.

#### Short title

##### 2

This Act may be cited as the Assembly Members (Reduction of Numbers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2016.
