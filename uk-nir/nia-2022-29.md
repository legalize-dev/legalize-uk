# General Teaching Council (Directions) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022

#### Power of Department to give directions

##### 1

- (1) The Education and Libraries (Northern Ireland) Order 1986 is amended as follows.

- (2) In Article 101 (power of Department to give directions), after sub-paragraph (f) of paragraph (3), insert—

> (fa) the General Teaching Council for Northern Ireland;

.

#### Commencement and short title

##### 2

- (1) This Act comes into operation on the day after receiving Royal Assent.

- (2) This Act may be cited as the General Teaching Council (Directions) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022.
