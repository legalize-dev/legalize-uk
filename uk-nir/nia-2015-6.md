# Ombudsman and Commissioner for Complaints (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2015 (repealed)

#### Extension of period for which acting Ombudsman and acting Commissioner may hold office

##### 1

- (1) In Article 6(2)(a) of the Ombudsman (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 (maximum period for which acting Ombudsman may hold office) for “12 months” substitute “24 months”.

- (2) In Article 5(2)(a) of the Commissioner for Complaints (Northern Ireland) Order 1996 (maximum period for which acting Commissioner may hold office) for “12 months” substitute “24 months”.

#### Retrospective operation of section 1

##### 2

The amendments made by section1 are to be regarded as having come into operation on 30 August 2014.

#### Commencement and short title

##### 3

- (1) This Act comes into operation on the day after it receives Royal Assent.

- (2) This Act may be cited as the Ombudsman and Commissioner for Complaints (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2015.
