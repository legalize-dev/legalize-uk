# Budget Act (Northern Ireland) 2001

#### Issue of sum out of the Consolidated Fund for year ending 31st March 2001 and appropriation of that sum

##### 1

- (1) The Department of Finance and Personnel may issue out of the Consolidated Fund and apply to the service of the year ending 31st March 2001 the sum of £195,599,000.

- (2) That sum is appropriated for the purposes specified in Schedule 1.

- (3) The sums already authorised to be appropriated in aid for that year shall be—

- (a) increased by the sums specified in the third column of Schedule 1 for the purposes in connection with which those sums are specified; and

- (b) decreased by the sums specified in the fourth column of Schedule 1 for the purposes in connection with which those sums are specified.

#### Issue of sum out of the Consolidated Fund for year ending 31st March 2002 and appropriation of that sum

##### 2

- (1) The Department of Finance and Personnel may issue out of the Consolidated Fund and apply to the service of the year ending 31st March 2002 the sum of £3,806,414,000.

- (2) That sum is appropriated for the purposes specified in Schedule 2.

#### Power of the Department of Finance and Personnel to borrow

##### 3

- (1) Subject to subsection (2), the Department of Finance and Personnel may borrow on the credit of the sums specified in sections 1 and 2 any sum or sums not exceeding in the whole £2,001,007,000.

- (2) Any money so borrowed shall be repaid, with any interest due thereon, out of the Consolidated Fund not later than 31st March 2002.

#### Use of resources for year ending 31st March 2002

##### 4

- (1) This section authorises the use of resources for the purposes of section 6(1) of the Government Resources and Accounts Act (Northern Ireland) 2001.

- (2) The use of resources by—

- (a) the Northern Ireland departments;

- (b) the Northern Ireland Assembly;

- (c) the Northern Ireland Audit Office;

- (d) the Assembly Ombudsman for Northern Ireland and the Northern Ireland Commissioner for Complaints; and

- (e) the Office for the Regulation of Electricity and Gas,

for the service of the year ending 31st March 2002 is authorised to the amount of £4,305,870,000.

- (3) In that year, resources may be used by a Northern Ireland department or a body or person mentioned in subsection (2) for the purposes specified in relation tothat department, body or person in column 1 of Schedule 3 up to the amounts specified in the corresponding entries in column 2.

#### Short title

##### 5

This Act may be cited as the Budget Act (Northern Ireland) 2001.
