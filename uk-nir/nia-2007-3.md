# Children (Emergency Protection Orders) Act (Northern Ireland) 2007

#### Repeal of Article 64(8) of the Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995

##### 1

In the [Children (Northern Ireland) Order 1995 (NI 2)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisi/1995/755) paragraph (8) of Article 64 (which prevents an application for the discharge of an emergency protection order being heard by a court within 72 hours of the making of the order) is repealed.

#### Short title and commencement

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Children (Emergency Protection Orders) Act (Northern Ireland) 2007.

- (2) This Act shall come into operation on Royal Assent.
