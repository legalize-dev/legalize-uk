# Allowances to Members of the Assembly (Repeal) Act (Northern Ireland) 2011

#### Repeals

##### 1

- (1) The Allowances to Members of the Assembly Act (Northern Ireland) [2000 (c. 3)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/nia/2000/3) is repealed.

- (2) The entry relating to the Northern Ireland Assembly Commission in Part 1 of the Schedule to the [Statutory Rules (Northern Ireland) Order 1979 (NI 12)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisi/1979/1573) is repealed.

#### Commencement

##### 2

This Act comes into operation on Royal Assent.

#### Short title

##### 3

This Act may be cited as the Allowances to Members of the Assembly (Repeal) Act (Northern Ireland) 2011.
