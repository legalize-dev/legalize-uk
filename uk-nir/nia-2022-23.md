# Fair Employment (School Teachers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022

#### Revocation of Article 71

##### 1

In the Fair Employment and Treatment (Northern Ireland) Order 1998 (“*the 1998 Order*”), omit Article 71 (school teachers).

#### Consequential amendments

##### 2

The Schedule to this Act has effect.

#### Commencement

##### 3

- (1) The preceding provisions of this Act come into operation on such day as the Executive Office may by order appoint (but this is subject to subsection (2)).

- (2) Those provisions come into operation at the end of the period of 2 years beginning with the day of Royal Assent if by the end of that period no order has been made under subsection (1) bringing them into operation.

- (3) An order under subsection (1) may include such transitional, transitory or saving provision as the Executive Office considers appropriate.

#### Short title

##### 4

This Act may be cited as the Fair Employment (School Teachers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022.

## SCHEDULE

### Fair Employment and Treatment (Northern Ireland) Order 1998

##### 1

The 1998 Order is amended as follows.

##### 2

In Article 18 (restriction on disclosure of information)—

- (a) in paragraph (3)(a) omit “or 71”; and

- (b) in paragraph (4)(a) omit “or 71(4)”.

##### 3

In Article 70 (excepted employments, etc.) omit paragraph (5).

##### 4

In Article 104(1) (regulations and orders) omit “, 71(6)”.

##### 5

In paragraph 1 of Schedule 2 (conduct of investigations by the Commission) omit “or 71”.

### Fair Employment and Treatment Order (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2003

##### 6

In the Fair Employment and Treatment Order (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2003, omit regulation 30 (amendment of Article 71 of the 1998 Order)
