# Scottish Local Authorities (Tendering) Act 2001

#### Compulsory competitive tendering: local authorities

##### 1

In section 2(10) of the Local Government Act [1988 (c. 9)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1988/9) (which empowers the Scottish Ministers to modify that Act’s competition provisions in their application to local authorities, but only up to 31 December 2001), the words “being a date not later than 31 December 2001,” are repealed.

#### Short title and commencement

##### 2

- (1) This Act may be cited as the Scottish Local Authorities (Tendering) Act 2001.

- (2) This Act shall come into force at the end of the period of one month beginning with the day on which it receives Royal Assent.
